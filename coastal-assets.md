## Santa Barbara Surf Culture Elements

### Typography Choices
- **Permanent Marker**: Hand-drawn surf culture headers
- **Caveat**: Relaxed, organic body text for casual vibe
- **Inter**: Clean readability for descriptions

### Color Palette (Organic Central Coast)
- **Ocean Blue**: #2e86ab (realistic Pacific blue)
- **Sand Warm**: #f7f3e3 (actual SB beach sand)
- **Kelp Green**: #4a5c3a (coastal vegetation)
- **Sunset Orange**: #f18f01 (SB golden hour)
- **Driftwood**: #8b7355 (weathered beach wood)

### Surf Spots Referenced
- **Rincon Point**: "Queen of the Coast" - iconic right point
- **Leadbetter/Harbor**: Protected beginner-friendly spots
- **Surf Beach**: Raw Lompoc power break
- **Morro Bay**: Classic Central Coast fishing/surfing

### Authentic Language
- "Dawn patrol" instead of "early morning"
- "Groms" for young surfers
- "Dialed" instead of "accurate"
- "Prime time" for optimal conditions
- "Sketchy" for dangerous conditions
