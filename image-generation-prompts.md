## Style Guidelines

### Color Palette:
- Ocean blues: #2e86ab, #4a90a4
- Sand tones: #f7f3e3, #e8dcc0  
- Sunset oranges: #f18f01
- Kelp green: #4a5c3a
- Driftwood brown: #8b7355

### Art Style:
- **Technique:** Watercolor with ink outlines
- **Mood:** Authentic surf culture, not corporate
- **Reference:** "Pete the Cat" style - bold outlines, flat fills, expressive but simple
- **Lighting:** Natural California coastal lighting
- **Atmosphere:** Salty, weathered, organic

### Technical Specs:
- **Format:** PNG with transparency or JPG
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI for web optimization
- **Aspect ratios:** Landscape orientation for all cards
- **File size:** Optimized for web (under 200KB each)

### Character Consistency:
- Same gray tabby markings across all images
- Navy hoodie with "Santa Barbara Surf Shop" text
- Consistent proportions and personality
- Relaxed, authentic surf culture poses
- Not overly cartoonish or commercial
